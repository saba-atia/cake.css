Delicious Chocolate Cake Recipe


What is Chocolate Cake

Choclate cake is a rich,maist dessert mase with cocoa powder,flour,eggs,and sugar.It's perfect any occasion,especially for chocalate lovers!


Ingredients
  • 13/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 cups sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 11/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 11/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoons salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup hot water

Steps to Prepare Chocolate Cake
  1. Preheat the oven to 350F (175C).Grease and flour tworound cake pans
  2. In a large mizing bowl,sift together the flour,sugar,cocoa powder,baking powder,baking soda,and salt.
  3. In another bowl,whisk the eggs,buttermilk,oil,and vanilla extract together.
  4. Gradually add the wet Ingredients to the dry Ingredients, stirring until smooth.
  5. Slowly add the hot water and mix well.The batter will be thin
  6. Pour the batter into the Prepared cake pans and bake dor 30-35 minutes
  7. Once done,let the cakes cool in the pans for 10 minutes,then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely
  8. Forst with your favorite chocolate frosting and serve!
